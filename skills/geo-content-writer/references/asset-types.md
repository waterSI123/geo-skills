# Asset Types

Map report-builder recommended assets and signals to these MVP asset types.

## comparison_article

Use for:

- `competitor_recommended_client_absent`
- competitor comparison topics
- `comparison article`
- `alternative page`

Goal: place the client brand into the buyer's comparison set without inventing competitor claims.

## category_landing_page

Use for:

- high-opportunity topics where the client is often absent
- `category landing page`
- `topic landing page`

Goal: establish category relevance and make the brand-topic association clear.

## compliance_policy_page

Use for:

- `policy_compliance_gap`
- `compliance page`
- `brand-guidelines workflow page`
- `platform-policy FAQ`

Goal: explain brand-guideline, policy-aware, and review workflow positioning without promising platform approval.

## proof_asset_page

Use for:

- `proof_asset_gap`
- `case study`
- `examples gallery`
- `pricing page`
- `integrations page`
- `reviews/testimonials section`

Goal: create a proof scaffold and mark missing evidence for the client to fill.

## faq_section

Use for:

- FAQ recommendations
- entity clarification
- weak recommendation
- policy or feature questions

Goal: create concise AI-readable answers about entity, category, users, use cases, proof, and alternatives.

## entity_clarification_content

Use for:

- `brand_existence_uncertain`
- `wrong_website`
- `wrong_category_needs_review`
- `wrong_feature_needs_review`
- `insufficient_brand_information`
- `entity validation page`
- `official profile updates`

Goal: clarify what the brand is, its official website, category, target users, and what claims need review.
