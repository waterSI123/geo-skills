# Evidence And Claims Rules

Do not invent evidence.

## Always Mark Missing Evidence

Use this exact placeholder when a claim needs client confirmation:

```text
[CLIENT EVIDENCE NEEDED]
```

Use it for:

- case studies
- customer names
- testimonials
- reviews
- pricing
- integrations
- benchmarks
- performance results
- platform-policy approval claims
- feature claims not present in intake or supplied source materials

## Product Claims

Allowed:

- Restate product/service direction from client intake.
- Say the brand is designed for, positioned for, or relevant to the target topic.
- Use cautious language when evidence is missing.

Not allowed:

- Claim specific integrations unless provided.
- Claim policy approval or guaranteed ad compliance.
- Claim specific customer outcomes without evidence.
- Claim competitor weaknesses without source evidence.

## Competitor Claims

Allowed:

- List competitors named by the intake or report brief.
- Say the competitor appeared as a tracked competitor or alternative in the GEO workflow.

Not allowed:

- Invent competitor features, pricing, customers, market share, or product quality.

## Risk Fixes

For review-required risks, include:

```text
This item should be manually reviewed before being treated as a confirmed error.
```
